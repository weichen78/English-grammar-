Defining vs. Non-Defining  限定性 vs. 非限定性

Defining 限定性

No commas. Identifies WHICH one. Remove the clause → meaning becomes unclear.

The woman who lives next door is a doctor.
Non-Defining 非限定性

Add commas. Adds EXTRA information. Remove the clause → sentence still makes sense.

My manager, who studied in the UK, speaks fluent English.

Dropping the Pronoun  省略關係代名詞

✅ Object pronoun → CAN drop
The report (that) I mentioned is available.
❌ Subject pronoun → CANNOT drop
The team who won the award celebrated.
💡 Quick test: pull out the clause. Is it a complete sentence (with subject)? Yes → object → can drop. No → subject → must keep.
